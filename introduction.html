What is SDG 7?

Sustainable Development Goal 7 (SDG 7) aims to ensure access to affordable, reliable, sustainable, and modern energy for all by 2030. Energy is the backbone of any economy, driving growth, innovation, and advancements in healthcare, education, and technology. However, over 770 million people still lack access to electricity, and many more rely on harmful, inefficient energy sources, contributing to global inequality and environmental degradation.
